INTRO

I’ve always been sort of a music snob, especially when it comes to the music played on the radio (like today’s top hits). Basically, I don’t listen to it. I think our generation as a collective is starting to feel the same way, especially with apps like Spotify taking over.

Nowadays, if you aren’t listening to the radio, you’re probably getting your music from your music subscription’s generated playlists, or your friends. While I have formed very strong relationships by sharing music, there still seems to be a WHOLE WORLD of music I haven’t explored.

I created Tempo as a platform for individuals to share their favorite music, as well as their thoughts about the music they listen to, in hopes that it will help those who seem trapped in their own little world of music.
