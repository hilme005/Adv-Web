As I’ve mentioned before, I don’t like the music on the radio. When a friend recommended artists like LANY and COIN, I thought “I know I’m not going to like this”. But, as a music aficionado, I gave it a shot. And now I’m hooked.

I personally don’t consider either LANY or COIN as alternative artists, but my friend insists. They definitely have songs that are ‘top hits radio’ -esque, so I categorize them as pop (google has my back on this one).

If you’re looking for chill pop vibes, LANY and COIN are definitely top contenders. They both have a range of upbeat and happy to slow and sad, and everything in between.
